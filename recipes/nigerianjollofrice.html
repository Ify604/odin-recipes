Nigerian Jollof Rice

Description

A classic Nigerian rice dish cooked in a rich tomato-pepper base.

Smoky, flavorful and perfect for any occasion.

All Ingredients

  • 3 cups long-grain parboiled rice.
  • 4 Large tomatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 3 shephards pepper
  • 2 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1 large onions
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 seasoning cube
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp thyme

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Blend the tomatoes, bell peper, shephard pepper, scotch bonnet pepper and half the onions until smooth.
  2. Heat the oil. Sautee sliced onions, then add the tomato paste and fry for 3 minutes.
  3. Pour in the blended mix. Fry on medium heat until the water reduces and the colour is deep red.
  4. Add the chicken stock, curry, thyme, salt and seasoning cube..
  5. Add the rinsed rice. Stir, cover and cook on low heat.
  6. Seam until the rice is soft and the liquid is absorbed.
  7. Fluff and serve.

Serving Suggestions

You can pair Nigerian Jollof Rice with:

  • Protein of choice
  • Fried plantain
  • MoiMoi
  • Coleslaw
  • Fried fish
