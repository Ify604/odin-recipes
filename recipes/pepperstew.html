Pepperstew

Description

A rich, spicy West African stew made with blended peppers, tomatoes and aromatics.

perfect with rice, pasta, yam, plantain or bread.

Simple,bold and always comforting.

All Ingredients

  • 6 Large tomatoes
  • 4 Red Bell Pepper
  • 3 Shephards Pepper
  • 2 Scotch Bonnet Pepper
  • 2 Large Onions
  • 3 CLoves of Garlic
  • 1 Thumb of Ginger
  • Protein choice (Chicken, beef or fish)
  • 1 Cup Vegetable oil
  • 2 Seasoning Cube
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp Curry Powder
  • 1 tsp Thyme

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Prep the pepper mix. Blend the tomatoes, bell peper, shephard pepper, scotch bonnet pepper, hald the garlic, onions and ginger until smooth.
  2. Season & cook the protein. If using chicken or beef, season the protein with salt, curry, thyme and onions. Boil until tender. Reserve the meatstock
  3. Fry the pepper base. Heat the oil. Sautee sliced onions, then pour in the blended mix. Fry on medium heat until the water reduces and the colour deepens.
  4. Add meat & stock. Add the cooked meat ans some meat stock and stir well.
  5. Season & simmer. Add seasoning cubes, curry, thyme and salt. SImmer for 15 - 20 minutes until thick and fragrant.
  6. Make final adjustments. Adjust salt, spice and thickness as needed.

Serving Suggestions

  • White rice
  • Jollof rice
  • Boiled yam
  • Fried plantain
  • Agege bread
