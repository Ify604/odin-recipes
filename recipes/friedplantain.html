Fried Plantains

Description

Sweet caramalized fried plantains. Crispy on the edges and soft inside.

A fast and popular Nigerian dish.

All Ingredients

  • 3 large plantains.
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Peel platains and slice diagonally or into rounds.
  2. Sprinkle slightly with salt (optional).
  3. Heat the oil in a pan ove medium heat.
  4. Fry planains on each side until golden brown.
  5. Remove and drain on paper towels

Serving Suggestions

You can pair fried plantain with:

  • Protein of choice
  • Nigerian jollof rice
  • White rice and stew
  • Fried eggs or egg sauce
  • Nigerian beans pottage
